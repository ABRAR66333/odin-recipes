Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a classic Indian dish featuring tender, marinated chicken in a rich, creamy tomato and butter sauce. Served with rice or naan, it's a flavorful and indulgent delight.

ingredients

  • Chicken pieces
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Garam masala
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Tomato puree
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt.
  2. Cook marinated chicken until done.
  3. Saut√© onions in butter, add garam masala, coriander powder, and tomato puree.
  4. Cook until oil separates, then add cooked chicken and simmer.
  5. Add cream, kasuri methi, and more butter.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander.
  7. Serve with rice or naan.
